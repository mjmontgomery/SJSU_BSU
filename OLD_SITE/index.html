Grad School Workshop Continuation

"Hello Grad school seekers,

The combined task forces of the AFAM/Black and Chicano/Latino organizations have had 2 grad school workshops so far hosted by the AFAM Student Success Intern's. In our last episode we split into 2 groups. One group went with Greg and the other with me. The group that went with Greg did part one whereas the group with me went on to part 2.

In an effort to catch everyone up to the same place we are doing part 2 (again) for those folks who had Greg last go round. The folks who had me can sit out and wait for part 3 on April 13th or are welcome to sit in on part 2 again. The workshop will take place in the Morris Dailey Auditorium at 7pm right after my class and run for 45 minutes. Bring a hard cover to write on since MD has no desks. We will supply handouts. Please be on time so we can leave on time. All are welcome. Thanks"

-Michael Randle
